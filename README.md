## Retail Sales Performance & Profitability Analysis (2024)
## Problem Statement
Businesses often generate large volumes of sales data but lack clear visibility into what is actually driving revenue, profitability, and operational performance.
This project addresses that gap by analyzing retail sales data to uncover key trends across regions, product categories, customer segments, and logistics.

## Objectives
- Identify key revenue and profit drivers
- Analyze regional and category-wise performance
- Evaluate customer segment contribution
- Assess operational efficiency (delivery time, returns)
- Provide actionable business recommendations

## Dataset
Total Orders: 5,000
Time Period: Jan 2024 – Dec 2024
Key Fields:
Region
Category & Product
Customer Segment
Shipping Mode
Sales, Profit, Orders

## Tools & Technologies
Excel: Data Cleaning & Transformation
Power BI: Dashboard Design & Visualization

## Dashboard Overview
The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of business performance through:
- KPI tracking (Sales, Profit, Orders, AOV, Return Rate)
- Sales trends across months
- Category and product-level performance
- Regional profit analysis
- Customer segmentation insights
- Shipping efficiency comparison
## Key Insights
- Revenue Concentration:
North, West, and South regions contribute ~75.3% of total revenue
- Category Dominance:
Electronics alone contributes ~59.7% of total sales
- Seasonality:
Sales peak in March, November, and December indicating strong seasonal demand
- Customer Segments:
Consumer segment contributes ~50% of revenue, making it the primary driver
- Operational Efficiency:
Economy shipping averages ~9 days compared to Same Day delivery (0 days)
- Return Rate:
Overall return rate stands at ~17.32%, indicating potential improvement areas

## Business Recommendations
- Regional Strategy:
Focus on improving performance in underperforming regions (Central, East, Northeast) through targeted marketing and product strategies
- Category Optimization:
Strengthen high-performing categories like Electronics while improving profitability in low-margin categories
- Customer Retention:
Prioritize high-value customer segments (Consumers) with loyalty and retention programs
- Logistics Improvement:
Reduce delivery timelines for Economy shipping to improve customer satisfaction and repeat purchases
- Return Reduction:
Investigate reasons behind high return rates and improve product quality or delivery accuracy
